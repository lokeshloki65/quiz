About SVHEC Erode

Shree Venkateshwara Hi-tech Engineering College is situated in Erode in Tamil Nadu state of India. Established in 2008, SVHEC is a Private UnAided college. The college is affiliated with Anna University and accredited by AICTE. Shree Venkateshwara Hi-tech Engineering College offers 12 courses across 2 streams namely Engineering, Management..Popular degrees offered at Shree Venkateshwara Hi-tech Engineering College include B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA..Besides a robust teaching pedagogy, Shree Venkateshwara Hi-tech Engineering College is also a leader in research and innovation.Focus is given to activities beyond academics at Shree Venkateshwara Hi-tech Engineering College, which is evident from its infrastructure, extracurricular activities and national & international collaborations. The placement at Shree Venkateshwara Hi-tech Engineering College is varied, with recruitment options both in corporates and public sector as well as entrepreneurship.
